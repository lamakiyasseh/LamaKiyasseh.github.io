Lama Kiyasseh

Country and Investment Risk, MIGA; formerly IFC, IMF; and Non-Resident Scholar, MEI

MEI - Economics and Energy Program

My name is Lama and I've spent over fourteen years working in economics and finance across the Middle East, Africa and the US. My interests include energy economics and policy, including the green transition, and infrastructure development.
